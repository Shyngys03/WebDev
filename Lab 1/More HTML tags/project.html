Omelette

  • Time: 5-10 minutes
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 3
Milk 100 ml
Salt 1 pinch

Step 1: The most important aspect in cooking - chicken eggs must be at room temperature! This is a prerequisite, so when planning to cook a magnificent omelette, take the eggs out of the refrigerator in advance and warm them at room temperature for 20-30 minutes.

Step 2: Crack the eggs into a deep bowl.

Step 3: Pour in the milk of the fat content of your choice.

Step 4: Add salt.

Step 5: Whisk the entire contents of the container with a whisk.

Step 6:Heat the pan on the stove, put the butter in it and melt it.

Step 6:Pour the egg mixture over the melted butter and immediately reduce the heat to low.

Step 6:Cover the pan with a lid and simmer the dish for about 10-15 minutes, until fully cooked.

Your omelette is done! Bon appetit!
